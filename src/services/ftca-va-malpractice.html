FTCA / VA Malpractice FAQ

What is the deadline for filing an FTCA claim?

You have two years from the date of injury to file your administrative claim (SF-95). Missing this deadline permanently bars your claim. There are very limited exceptions, so immediate action is critical.

Can I sue the VA for medical malpractice?

Yes, but only through the FTCA process. You cannot sue VA doctors individually - the federal government is the only defendant. The process is complex and requires experienced FTCA counsel.

How much can I recover in an FTCA case?

FTCA allows recovery of medical expenses, lost wages, pain and suffering, and other damages. However, punitive damages are not available, and the government often disputes damage calculations aggressively.
